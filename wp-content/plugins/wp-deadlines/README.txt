Finally, A countdown timer to increase your sales and email conversions by creating a urgency to your buyer.

== Description ==

Wondering why your sales page isn’t converting or your marketing email isn’t getting the click rate it deserve?

You are doing all wrong…

Every successful campaign create urgency to their buyer so they can act fast and close the deal. Here comes WP Deadlines.

WP Deadlines will display a highly customizable countdown timer to your e-commerce(woo commerce) page, side-wide flyout banner and countdown timers for your email.

No need to use an extra plugin for countdown timer on site and SaaS service for email. Everything is now combined in one plugin and its 100% free and open source.

By using a 3rd party countdown timer inside your email you are compromising your email credibility because all email service provider(Gmail, Outlook, AOL) mark your email as spam if you use any link other than your domain.

Wp Deadlines is useful for :

* Creating urgency for marketing campaign.
* Increase email conversion rate.
* Ticket selling by creating urgency.
* Fund raising campaign.
* Webinar registration page.
* Course selling.
* Evergreen webinar registration page.
* And much more…


== Features ==

* Create unlimited Countdowns.
* Deadline countdown.
* Evergreen countdown + cookie tracking.
* Flyout countdown for side wide and position them top/bottom.
* Create Countdown for pages/posts.
* Create Countdown widgets.
* Generate Shortcode to use everywhere.
* Use countdown for your email with our dynamic gif animation generator.
* Use custom fonts, colors, backgrounds to style your Countdown Timer.
* Ability to type your own text in Countdown day field
* Ability to type your own text in Countdown hour field
* Ability to type your own text in Countdown minute field
* Ability to type your own text in Countdown second field
* Ability to type message after countdown timer expired

== Installation ==

1. Upload `wp-deadlines` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
